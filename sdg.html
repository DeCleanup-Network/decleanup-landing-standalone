SDG ALIGNMENT · OFFICIAL BRIEF

Aligned with the
United Nations
Sustainable Development Goals.

DeCleanup Rewards on Base and the DeCleanup Network protocol turn local cleanup actions into verifiable, onchain proof. A primitive that maps directly to five Goals.

SDG 11 SDG 12 SDG 13 SDG 14 SDG 15
11
GOAL ELEVEN

Sustainable Cities & Communities

Make cities and human settlements inclusive, safe, resilient and sustainable.

OUR LINK ↦ Verified neighborhood cleanups give cities transparent waste data & reward residents for civic action.
12
GOAL TWELVE

Responsible Consumption & Production

Ensure sustainable consumption and production patterns.

OUR LINK ↦ Every cleanup categorizes waste (PET, plastic, glass, mixed), feeding open data on what's actually being thrown out, where.
13
GOAL THIRTEEN

Climate Action

Take urgent action to combat climate change and its impacts.

OUR LINK ↦ Cleanup actions become hypercerts and feed dMRV pipelines, so climate-aligned funders get auditable receipts, not estimates.
14
GOAL FOURTEEN

Life Below Water

Conserve and sustainably use the oceans, seas and marine resources.

OUR LINK ↦ Beach & harbor cleanups stop plastic at the coast before it enters the ocean. HEM Japan ran 9 such events in 2024.
15
GOAL FIFTEEN

Life on Land

Protect, restore and promote sustainable use of terrestrial ecosystems.

OUR LINK ↦ Forest, riverbank and roadside cleanups reduce litter that fragments habitats and poisons soil. Pestathon ran 4 campus & community events in Nigeria, 2024.
